How to download Teachable

1.Please add this Chrome extension to your Chrome browser.
video downloader - CocoCut

2.Press the jigsaw icon in the upper right corner of your Chrome browser and pin CocoCut.

3.Open the page containing the Teachable video you want to download.

4.Press the palm tree icon.

5.Press the Force Download button.

6.Wait until the video conversion process is finished.

7.Press the SAVE button to download the video.
